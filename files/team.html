Nicole "Sassy" Harrison

Founder & CEO — Creative Direction, Strategy & Vision

820 Collective grew out of years of doing the work before ever calling it a service. I've always been the person people turned to when they needed clarity, direction, or a fresh perspective on an idea they couldn't quite articulate.

Over time, that support evolved into something more intentional. Today, 820 Collective exists to help individuals and brands define their voice, organize their ideas, and move forward with confidence — without forcing themselves into a box that doesn't fit.

My goal is simple: help people move forward with confidence and purpose.
